# Freshness Policy

Runtime packet generation uses curated fixtures. Live APIs are smoke/refresh
helpers unless explicitly enabled and reviewed.

Freshness targets:

- Food, shelter, legal aid, WIC, crisis, and urgent resources: 7 days or before
  demo freeze.
- County/city office contacts: 14-30 days.
- Program pages: 30-60 days or before demo freeze.
- Annual federal references: annually.

Local handoffs must include call-before-going and must not claim live
availability.
