# Disallowed Claims

These claims block packet export:

- "You qualify"
- "You are eligible"
- "Guaranteed"
- "Benefit amount"
- "You will receive"
- "We submitted"
- "Application submitted"
- "Enter your SSN"
- "Upload your SSN"
- "Provide your password"
- "Shelter bed is available"
- "Legal advice"

Allowed framing:

- "Likely worth checking"
- "Needs more information"
- "Local handoff recommended"
- "Prepare these documents if available"
- "Call or confirm before going"
- "An official agency or local office makes the final decision"
