# Crisis, Domestic Violence, And Urgent Handoffs

Crisis and domestic-violence routes suppress the normal benefits packet by
default.

Routes:

- `crisis`: self-harm, immediate danger, overdose, medical emergency, or acute
  safety emergency. Use fixed handoff language.
- `domestic_violence`: domestic violence, stalking, trafficking, or unsafe home
  context. Use fixed handoff language and avoid exact location disclosure.
- `urgent`: food today, shelter tonight, shutoff, eviction, or urgent loss of
  basic needs. Continue prep only after immediate handoff notes.
- `standard`: normal benefits-prep path.

Do not claim live shelter or food availability. Always include call-before-going
for urgent local resources.
