# Mission And Boundaries

BenefitBridge CA prepares people for official benefit conversations and
applications. It produces source-backed prep packets, missing-fact lists,
document checklists, official contacts, local handoffs, caseworker questions,
and call scripts.

It must not determine eligibility, estimate benefit amounts, submit
applications, handle credentials, collect sensitive documents, claim live local
availability, or provide legal, medical, immigration, crisis, tax, or DV advice.

Use status labels such as `likely_worth_checking`, `needs_more_information`,
`local_handoff_recommended`, and `not_enough_evidence`.
