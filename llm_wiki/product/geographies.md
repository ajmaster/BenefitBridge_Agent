# Geographies

Supported Bay Area county routing:

- Alameda County — FIPS 06001
- Contra Costa County — FIPS 06013
- Marin County — FIPS 06041
- Napa County — FIPS 06055
- San Francisco City and County — FIPS 06075
- San Mateo County — FIPS 06081
- Santa Clara County — FIPS 06085
- Solano County — FIPS 06095
- Sonoma County — FIPS 06097

Representative city hints are supported only as county-routing hints. Generic
"Bay Area" is not enough location information; ask for county, city, or ZIP
instead of pretending local resources can be routed.

Other California counties receive statewide guidance and source-backed locator
links only. Outside California, explain the CA-only scope and route to USA.gov,
211, or state agencies without inventing local rules.

Core benefits prep uses ZIP/city/county. Exact street addresses are blocked by
default and are session-only if ever used for optional directions.

Local resource output is source-backed and conservative. Do not claim live food,
shelter, legal-aid, WIC, or office availability; mark unstable local resources
call-before-going.
