# User Workflows

Primary MVP workflows:

- Prepare for food help: CalFresh, WIC where facts indicate it may be relevant,
  food bank or 211 handoffs.
- Prepare before calling: one-page situation summary, documents, questions, and
  call script.
- Lost work hours: food, health, utility, and housing-stability prep paths.
- Document checklist: conceptual checklist only; no real uploads in demo.
- Plain-language rewrite: English and Spanish with caveats preserved.
- Local help: curated resources with call-before-going and no availability
  guarantees.
