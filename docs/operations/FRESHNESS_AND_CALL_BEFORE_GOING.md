# Freshness And Call-Before-Going

Live public APIs and local resources can change. Runtime packet generation uses
frozen fixtures and labels unstable handoffs conservatively.

Freshness windows:

- Urgent food, shelter, legal aid, WIC, crisis, and local resources: 7 days or
  before demo freeze.
- County/city office contacts: 14-30 days.
- Program pages: 30-60 days.
- Annual federal references: annually.

Call-before-going is required for:

- Shelter or housing access.
- Food pantry or food-bank handoffs.
- WIC office or clinic handoffs.
- Legal aid handoffs.
- Office hours or appointment-sensitive resources.

Do not claim live availability.
