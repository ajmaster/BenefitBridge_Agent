# BenefitBridge CA — sample prep packet

**Synthetic profile:** San Francisco adult without dependent children, very low/no income, needs food, health coverage, cash assistance, and shelter handoff.

## Safety notice

BenefitBridge does not determine eligibility, estimate benefit amounts, or submit applications. Agencies decide eligibility. Call before going to any food, shelter, clinic, or legal-aid location.

## Potential benefit paths

### Food: CalFresh / free food locations

**Status:** likely worth checking.

**Where to start:** SFHSA CalFresh apply page, BenefitsCal, SFHSA free food locations, SF-Marin Food Bank locator.

### Health: Medi-Cal

**Status:** likely worth checking.

**Where to start:** SFHSA Medi-Cal, phone 855-355-5757, or BenefitsCal.

### Cash assistance: CAAP

**Status:** likely worth checking if adult without dependent children and San Francisco resident.

**Where to start:** SFHSA CAAP page and BenefitsCal. Do not assume eligibility.

### Shelter / homelessness services

**Status:** local handoff recommended.

**Where to start:** San Francisco Access Points / Adult Shelter Access Line. No live bed availability is claimed.

## Caseworker questions

1. Which benefits should I apply for first based on my current situation?
2. If I do not have all documents, can I still start an application?
3. Where should I go or call for shelter access today?
4. Can I receive help in my preferred language?
5. How do I keep contact information current if I do not have stable housing?
