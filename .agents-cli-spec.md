# Agent Spec

## Overview

BenefitBridge CA is a California-only benefits preparation and handoff assistant
for Santa Clara County, San Jose, and San Francisco. It produces source-backed
prep packets with possible benefit areas worth checking, missing facts,
document checklists, official links, local handoffs, caseworker questions, and
printable/exportable summaries.

It is not an eligibility determiner, benefits calculator, application submitter,
credential handler, legal advisor, medical advisor, immigration advisor, crisis
counselor, or live local-resource availability system.

## Example Use Cases

- A Spanish-speaking single parent in San Jose prepares for food, health, and
  utility assistance conversations.
- A San Jose adult needs food today and shelter/housing handoff tonight.
- A San Francisco adult prepares for food, health coverage, cash assistance, and
  housing help.
- A user asks whether WIC applies but omits child ages or pregnancy/postpartum
  facts.
- A user provides SSN, credentials, or exact sensitive location details and the
  agent redacts/refuses before continuing with non-sensitive fields.

## Tools Required

- `lookup_county_from_location`: ZIP/city/county to jurisdiction routing.
- `get_county_profile`: pilot county/city contacts and handoffs.
- `retrieve_approved_source` and `search_source_snapshot`: approved citation
  metadata only.
- `get_benefit_program_area` and `match_benefit_paths`: deterministic prep-area
  matching without eligibility decisions.
- `find_local_resources`: curated local handoffs with call-before-going.
- `get_healthcare_gov_content`, `search_hud_housing_counselors`,
  `query_datasf_socrata`: refresh/smoke boundaries, not runtime-critical.
- `redact_pii`, `validate_packet`, `translate_packet`, `export_packet`:
  guardrail and packet lifecycle tools.

## Constraints & Safety Rules

- No eligibility guarantees, benefit amount estimates, application submission,
  credential handling, case-status access, or sensitive document upload.
- No SSNs, EBT/PINs, case numbers, birthdates, credentials, real IDs,
  immigration documents, or exact safety-sensitive locations in model context,
  packet exports, or telemetry.
- DV, stalking, trafficking, youth safety, crisis, and immediate danger route to
  fixed handoff language and suppress normal packet details by default.
- Every benefit path must cite approved sources. Invented URLs, unsupported
  program names, stale critical sources, and prohibited phrases block export.
- English and Spanish are MVP. Other languages are draft/roadmap only unless
  reviewed.

## Success Criteria

- `uv run pytest tests/unit tests/integration` passes.
- `agents-cli info` recognizes the project with `agent_directory: app`.
- Eval corpus starts from gold profiles, red-team tests, and expanded profiles.
- Critical eval gates target 100% safety, citation, schema, no invented URLs, no
  eligibility guarantees, no benefit amounts, and no PII leakage.
